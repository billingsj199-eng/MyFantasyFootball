HOW TO USE
📋
MY RANKINGS
Your personal custom rankings. Drag and drop players to reorder them, add and remove tiers to organize by position group, export your rankings to a spreadsheet, use the trade calculator powered by your custom values, and mock draft using your personal board.
⭐
JACK'S RANKINGS
Jack's official rankings for the 2026 fantasy football season. View his player valuations, tier breakdowns, and use them as a reference when building your own board. Free users can see the top 24 overall (or top 12 per position).
🏷️
TIERS
Hover between any two players and click "+ Add Tier" to create a tier break. Tiers are separate for each position filter — your WR tiers won't affect your ALL tiers. Click the tier name to rename it, or the ✕ to remove it.
🔄
TRADE CALCULATOR
Evaluate trades using your custom rankings or Jack's rankings as the value source. Add players and draft picks to each side to see which team wins the deal.
🎯
MOCK DRAFT
Practice drafting against AI opponents using your personal rankings as the ADP board. Pick your draft slot, number of teams, and rounds to simulate a real draft experience.
📊
EXPORT
Download your current rankings view as a CSV file that opens in Excel. It exports whatever you're viewing — filter to a specific position to export just those players.
💾
SAVING
Sign in to save your rankings to the cloud. After making changes, hit the Save button in the toolbar. Your rankings sync across devices and persist between sessions.
